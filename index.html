My current research is focused on non-Newtonian flow and dynamic optimization for 3D printing applications. My research group at UC Berkeley can found at Multiphysics Simulation and Optimization Lab. Outside of my everyday research I train as an endurance athlete. You can find me on social media for my various interests: GitHub, Strava, films, and Twitter.
